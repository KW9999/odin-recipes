Easy Egg Fried Rice

Description

This amazingly simple egg fried rice from Aaron and Claire will revolutionize how you think about fried rice! Not only does it take 5 minutes to prepare, but you can add things like shrimp, bacon, and even kimchi to take your fried rice game to the next level!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • salt
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • cooking oil
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs in a mixing bowl and beat
  2. Add 1 cup of cooked rice and stir
  3. Add 1 tbsp of oyster sauce and 1 tbsp of cooking oil, stir again
  4. Chop green onion
  5. Add the white part of the green onion to some oil and heat on medium, until aromatic
  6. Once aromatic, switch to high heat and add rice
  7. Stir until well coated and cook to preference
  8. add optional ingredients during this time
