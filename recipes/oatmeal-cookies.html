Oatmeal Cookies

Description

These chewy and delicious oatmeal cookies are sure to be a crowd pleaser! The best part is - the ingredients couldn't be easier. Enjoy these delcious treats for dessert, or even paired with coffee for a yummy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/6 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tbsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1/3 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1.5 cups oats
  • 3/4 cup raisins

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Beat butter until creamy
  3. Add brown and granulated sugar and beat until fluffy (about 2 minutes)
  4. Beat in eggs
  5. Beat in vanilla
  6. In a seperate bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon
  7. Mix dry ingredients into the wet ingredients on low speed
  8. Bake for 9-13 minutes or until golden brown
