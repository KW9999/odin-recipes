Lasagna

Description

A simple yet classic lasagna recipe that will remind you of your childhood. Whole wheat noodles are used to give the lasagna a light yet savory flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 package cottage cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 16oz package lasagna noodles
  • 8oz shredded mozzerella cheese
