Lasagna

Description

A simple yet classic lasagna recipe that will remind you of your childhood. Whole wheat noodles are used to give the lasagna a light yet savory flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 package cottage cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 16oz package lasagna noodles
  • 8oz shredded mozzerella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add mushrooms and onions; saute until onions are transparent. Stir in pasta sauce and heat through
  3. In medium sized bowl, combine cottage cheese, ricotta cheese, grated parmesan cheese, and eggs
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 13x9 inch pan. Layer with uncooked lasagna noodles, cheese mixture, mozarella cheese, and meat sauce. Continue layering until all Ingredients are used, reserving 1/2 cup mozarella. Cover pan with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in pregeated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover, and top with remaining half cup of mozzarella cheese. Bake for an additional 15 minutes. Remove from oven, and let stand 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
